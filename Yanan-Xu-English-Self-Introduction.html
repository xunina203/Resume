Yanan Xu | Interview Self-Introduction

Supply Chain Planning · Material Management · Project Delivery

2-3 Minute Version

Hello, my name is Yanan Xu. I have over 10 years of experience in supply chain planning, material management, and project delivery, mainly in consumer electronics, smart hardware, and engineering project environments. I have been involved in projects for Apple iPhone, Meta AR/VR, and Xiaomi ecosystem products.

I started my career at Foxconn, where I was responsible for purchasing execution and material control. During that time, I supported six Apple iPhone new product introduction projects, covering material planning, CTB readiness, shortage management, supplier delivery follow-up, and the transition from NPI to mass production. This experience helped me build a solid foundation in supply chain operations and material management under fast-paced and high-standard project environments.

Later, I worked at Opple Lighting as a delivery project manager, where I was responsible for material planning, delivery schedules, and cross-functional coordination for engineering projects. This role expanded my experience from manufacturing-side material management to end-to-end project delivery and on-site coordination.

After that, I worked as a material project manager on Meta AR/VR projects. I managed material planning and readiness for AR/VR glasses projects, involving more than 1,200 BOM components, over 50 suppliers, and 3 contract manufacturers. I coordinated delivery schedules, inventory issues, quality exceptions, and cost optimization activities. This experience strengthened my ability to manage complex BOM structures, multiple suppliers, and multi-site manufacturing coordination.

Currently, I work at Xiaomi as a material planning manager, leading a team of seven people. My scope covers core electronic components, centralized procurement materials, strategic material preparation, BOM transitions, inventory governance, and system-based process improvement. For example, I have supported annual centralized procurement transactions of around RMB 6.03 billion, covering thousands of SKUs. I also helped build a strategic material preparation mechanism from scratch and supported GSC system rule optimization to reduce obsolete inventory risks and improve team efficiency.

Overall, I would summarize my strengths in three areas. First, I have strong experience in new product material management from NPI to mass production, especially for complex BOMs and critical components. Second, I am good at cross-functional coordination with internal teams, suppliers, contract manufacturers, and project stakeholders to drive issue closure. Third, I value data-driven management and process improvement, and I am willing to use system tools and AI tools to improve planning accuracy and operational efficiency.

Going forward, I would like to continue developing in supply chain planning, material management, or project delivery. I hope to join a platform with high business complexity and high standards for delivery quality and efficiency, where I can further contribute my experience in critical material management, supply risk control, inventory governance, and team management.
